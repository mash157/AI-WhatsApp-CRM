⚡ CRON JOBS (Automated Tasks)

1. Inactivity Follow-up
   - Runs: Every 6 hours
   - Action: Send reminder to inactive users
   - Interval: 24 hours of inactivity

2. Booking Reminders
   - Runs: Every hour
   - Action: Notify customers before appointments
   - Interval: 24 hours before booking

3. Payment Reminders
   - Runs: Daily at 9 AM
   - Action: Send payment pending alerts
   - Interval: Based on payment status

4. Trial Expiry Alerts
   - Runs: Daily at 12 PM
   - Action: Remind users about expiring trials
   - Interval: 7 days before expiry

5. Usage Reset
   - Runs: Monthly (1st day at 12 AM)
   - Action: Reset usage counters
   - Interval: Every 30 days
